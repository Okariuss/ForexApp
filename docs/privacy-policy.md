# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

ForexApp does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

## Data Collection

ForexApp does not collect any personal information from users.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party identity services.

## Network Requests

ForexApp connects to ExchangeRate-API to fetch current foreign exchange rates.

These requests are used only to retrieve exchange rate data. ForexApp does not send personal information, account information, contact information, location data, or device identifiers to ExchangeRate-API.

The selected base currency may be included in the exchange rate request so the app can display conversion rates.

## Local Storage

ForexApp stores the user's selected base currency locally on the device using UserDefaults.

This information remains on the user's device and is not transmitted by ForexApp for tracking, analytics, advertising, or profiling.

## Permissions

ForexApp does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as location, camera, microphone, photos, contacts, calendars, or health data.

## Tracking

ForexApp does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

## Children's Privacy

ForexApp does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
